Photography

One way I channel my creativity is through photography. The photo of water shown as well as the photo above are only some of the many photos I've taken (More photos to be displayed on website soon)! I've hosted multiple photo shows as well as won awards such as the National Silver Medal for Photography from Scholastic Arts and Writing, and was a YoungArts Winner in 2016.
As a photographer, I believe that the scenes people pass every day are underappreciated. I aim to shoot pictures to expose the unique characteristics within a scene: texture, shape, color, contrast and structure. By pointing out the fascinating qualities of the seemingly mundane, I hope to offer viewers a new perspective of their surroundings.
